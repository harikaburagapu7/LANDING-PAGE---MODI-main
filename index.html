About Narendra Modi

Narendra Modi, the 14th and current Prime Minister of India, has been serving the nation since May 2014. Known for his dynamic leadership and innovative policies, Modi has been a significant force in shaping modern India. His journey from a humble beginning to becoming the leader of the world's largest democracy is truly inspiring.

Achievements

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

Launched on October 2, 2014, this nationwide campaign aims to clean up the streets, roads, and infrastructure of India's cities and rural areas.

Digital India

This initiative focuses on transforming India into a digitally empowered society and knowledge economy by ensuring internet connectivity and digital literacy.

Make in India

Launched to encourage national and multinational companies to manufacture their products in India, boosting employment and the economy.

Ayushman Bharat

This health initiative aims to provide accessible healthcare to millions of people, ensuring health insurance and wellness centers across the country.

Initiatives

Smart Cities Mission

A vision to drive economic growth and improve the quality of life of people by enabling local area development and harnessing technology.

Pradhan Mantri Ujjwala Yojana

An initiative to provide LPG connections to women from Below Poverty Line (BPL) households, promoting cleaner cooking fuels.

Beti Bachao Beti Padhao

A campaign to generate awareness and improve the efficiency of welfare services intended for girls in India.
